Hello and welcome to Poplar Grove Acres. We are a small family farm in south central Kentucky. We raise Angus Beef Cattle and Nigerian Dwarf Goats. We strive to provide quality care and management to our livestock, and are blessed each day for the ability to grow and improve. On our site you will be able to follow along on our growing journey of farming and developing a more self-sustaining lifestyle. Along with educational resources you can utilize to learn more about Nigerian Dwarf Goats. I hope you enjoy!!!
